8-Week Progression

Weeks 1–2Master form. Use 2kg only. Do knee push-ups. Focus on breathing in yoga.
Weeks 3–4Add 1–2 reps per set. Try 1–2 full push-ups at end of each set.
Weeks 5–6Introduce 5kg for squats + deadlifts. Increase treadmill speed by 0.5 km/h.
Weeks 7–8Move to 5kg for upper body rows. Aim for 10 full push-ups in a row.

Equipment

2kg dumbbells · 5kg dumbbells · Treadmill · Yoga mat · Yoga block · Bench

Sudarshan Kriya is done at home after returning from gym. Wait 10–15 min after exercise before starting pranayama.
